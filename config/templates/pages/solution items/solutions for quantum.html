Solutions for Quantum Computers

To implement quantum algorithms, a set of unitary gates must be realized with high fidelity with every possible longer coherence time. In a real experiment with atomic (ionic), spin-based, or superconductor-based quantum computing issues such as decoherence control, error mitigation, and dynamic decoupling must be addressed. In QuCTech we address all aspects of quantum computers such as quantum algorithms designed to formulate in the language of quantum algorithms and solve intractable problems and applications, the realization of gate-based quantum algorithms, derivation of suitable high-fidelity long coherence time quantum control strategy for the realization of quantum gates of quantum circuits of quantum algorithms, readouts, quantum interconnections, quantum error corrections. In general, our mission is to design and build full-stack quantum operating systems (QOS) to carry out both classical and quantum user tasks, and to be agnostic to physical qubit platforms (atom, ion, semiconductor, superconductor qubits architecture, or any other platforms).
