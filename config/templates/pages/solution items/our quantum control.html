Our Qubits Control Strategies

We produced Lyapunov feedback control, optimal control, and deep-learning-based quantum control software tools both for closed quantum systems and open quantum systems. Our tools for analysis and control of open quantum systems divide into two categories non-stochastic quantum master equations and stochastic open quantum systems (stochastic master equations and stochastic differential equations). Our quantum optimal control contains both state steering control and realization control for the implementation of unitary matrices of quantum information architectures.

An example schematic of quantum open system control: (a)- state steering control of quantum system under continuous measurement which is described by quantum trajectory or quantum filtering or stochastic Schrödinger equations. (b)- measurement or output feedback-based control of open quantum systems. (c)- coherent fully quantum feedback. (d)- deep learning-based modeling and control of many-body qubit systems.
